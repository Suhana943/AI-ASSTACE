Laptop Lete Waqt Hone Wali Galtiyan

LaptopTec experts ne observe kiya hai ki aksar log naye laptop lete waqt ye galtiyan karte hain:

  • Sirf Processor par focus karna: Aksar log sirf i5 ya Ryzen 5 jaise processor ke naam par laptop khareed lete hain, jabki laptop ki asli speed aur smoothness ke liye RAM aur SSD ki speed zaroori hoti hai.
  • Zaroorat se alag laptop chuna: Office ya college ke liye heavy gaming laptop le lena ek badi galti hoti hai, kyunki gaming laptops bhari hote hain aur unki battery life kaafi kam hoti hai.
  • Sirf kam price dekhna: Sasta laptop lene ke chakkar mein log aisi build quality aur components wala device le lete hain jo sirf kuch mahino mein hi slow hone lagta hai.
  • Screen quality ko nazar-andaz karna: Jo log lambe samay tak screen par kaam karte hain, woh aksar display panel ki quality check nahi karte, jisse aankhon par zor pad sakta hai.
