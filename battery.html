🔋 Battery Optimization & Buying Guide

How to extend battery life?

  • Lower the Refresh Rate from 144Hz to 60Hz inside display properties.
  • Turn on Windows auto-battery saver mode at 30% dynamically.
  • Close background processing applications to prevent power drain.

Questions to ask before buying:

  • What is the exact Watt-hour (Whr) capacity? Look for 45Whr to 60Whr.
  • Does it support Type-C Fast Charging standard protocol?
  • Is the processor optimized on an energy-saving architecture?
