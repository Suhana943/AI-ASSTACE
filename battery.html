🔋 Laptop Battery Optimization & Buying Guide

Bina charger ke lambe samay tak kaam karne ke liye sahi processor aur sahi settings ka hona zaroori hai. Niche diye gaye parameters ko zaroor check karein:

How to extend battery life?

  • Lower the Refresh Rate: Screen refresh rate ko 144Hz se hatakar 60Hz par set karein taaki battery kam consume ho.
  • Use Battery Saver Mode: Windows settings mein jaakar auto-battery saver ko 20% ya 30% par turn-on rakhein.
  • Close Background Apps: Heavy background programs aur unused browser tabs ko hamesha close kar dein.

Questions to ask before buying:

  • What is the total Watt-hour (Whr) capacity of the laptop's battery? (Prefer 45Whr to 60Whr inside this budget)
  • Does it support Type-C Fast Charging for quick emergency top-ups?
  • Is the processor U-series or Vostro energy-saving architecture?
