The Dell XPS 13 (2024) continues to push the boundaries of ultraportable design with its minimalist aesthetic and premium CNC aluminum chassis. Powered by the latest Intel Core Ultra processors, this laptop integrates advanced AI capabilities directly into your daily workflow while maintaining incredible power efficiency. Whether you are a professional on the go or a student seeking top-tier performance, the XPS 13 offers a compelling glimpse into the future of compact computing.

✅ Pros

  • Stunning, ultra-modern chassis design with a seamless glass haptic touchpad.
  • Smooth 120Hz variable refresh rate display offers fluid visuals.
  • Strong battery life paired with efficient Intel Core Ultra performance.

❌ Cons

  • Extremely limited port selection with only two Thunderbolt 4 ports.
  • Capacitive touch function row lacks physical feedback and takes time to get used to.
  • Absence of a dedicated 3.5mm headphone jack.

⚖️ Verdict

The Dell XPS 13 (2024) remains a triumph of industrial design, offering unmatched portability combined with cutting-edge Intel Core Ultra performance. While its minimalist port selection and futuristic capacitive touch keys require some user adaptation, it stands out as one of the most premium and forward-thinking Windows ultraportables available today.

💡 Pro-Tip: If you frequently work with creative visual media, opt for the OLED display configuration, as the contrast levels and color depth are vastly superior to the base FHD+ model.

Rating: 4.5/5
