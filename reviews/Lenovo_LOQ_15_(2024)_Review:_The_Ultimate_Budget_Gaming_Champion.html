Lenovo LOQ 15 (2024) Review: The Ultimate Budget Gaming Champion

Price: ₹79,990

🛒 View on Amazon India

The budget gaming laptop segment has a new king, and it goes by the name of Lenovo LOQ 15 (2024). Borrowing heavily from the premium Legion DNA, this machine brings high-tier performance to a price point that doesn't break the bank. Powered by the Intel Core i5-13450HX processor and paired with an NVIDIA RTX 4060 GPU, it handles modern AAA titles with surprising ease. While it cuts a few corners in chassis materials to keep costs down, the thermals and display quality punch far above their weight. For students and entry-level gamers in India, this laptop represents the absolute sweet spot of price and performance.

ProcessorIntel Core i5-13450HX (10 Cores, 16 Threads, up to 4.60 GHz)
RAM16GB DDR5-4800MHz (Dual Channel, expandable up to 32GB)
Storage512GB SSD M.2 2242 PCIe Gen4 NVMe
Display15.6-inch FHD (1920x1080) IPS, 144Hz, 100% sRGB, G-Sync, 300 nits
Battery60Whr with 170W Slim Tip adapter (Super Rapid Charge Pro)

Pros

  • Exceptional raw gaming performance with 115W TGP RTX 4060
  • Gorgeous 100% sRGB screen is a massive upgrade in this budget class
  • Robust dual-fan cooling system prevents thermal throttling under load
  • Excellent keyboard ergonomics with comfortable travel and tactile feedback

Cons

  • Subpar battery life, lasting barely 3 to 4 hours on non-gaming casual tasks
  • Chassis is entirely built of plastic and tends to attract grease and fingerprints

Verdict

The Lenovo LOQ 15 (2024) is a triumphant entry-level gaming machine that redefines expectations in the mid-range segment. It successfully prioritizes pure performance, cooling efficiency, and display accuracy over premium materials.
