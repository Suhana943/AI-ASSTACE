Lenovo IdeaPad Pro 5i Gen 9 (14-inch, 2024) Review: The Ultimate Mid-Range Powerhouse

Price: ₹1,04,990

🛒 View on Amazon India

The mid-range laptop segment has become increasingly competitive, with manufacturers striving to deliver premium features at accessible price points. Enter the Lenovo IdeaPad Pro 5i Gen 9 (14-inch, 2024), a device that promises to bridge the gap between everyday productivity and high-performance computing. Boasting the latest Intel Core Ultra processors and a stunning high-refresh-rate OLED display, this machine aims to appeal to both professionals and casual creators. Its sleek aluminum chassis immediately gives off a premium vibe that rivals much more expensive ultrabooks on the market today. Beyond its attractive exterior, Lenovo has packed this ultraportable with an impressive array of ports and robust internal cooling. During our extensive testing, the laptop handled demanding multitasking sessions and moderate creative workloads without breaking a sweat. While it may not carry the prestigious ThinkPad or Yoga branding, it quietly delivers performance that matches, and sometimes exceeds, its more expensive siblings. Battery life is also a notable highlight, easily getting us through a standard workday on a single charge thanks to smart power optimizations. Ultimately, the IdeaPad Pro 5i Gen 9 emerges as an exceptionally well-rounded contender that redefines what a sub-one-lakh premium laptop can achieve.

Pros

  • Stunning 120Hz 2.8K OLED display with exceptional color accuracy.
  • Powerful Intel Core Ultra 7 processor delivers robust multitasking and AI capabilities.
  • Excellent port selection including Thunderbolt 4, HDMI 2.1, and a full-size SD card reader.
  • Impressive 84Wh battery life that easily outlasts many competitors in its class.
  • Sturdy, premium all-aluminum chassis build quality.

Cons

  • The stereo speakers lack deep bass and can sound slightly tinny at high volumes.
  • RAM is soldered and cannot be upgraded post-purchase.
  • The 1080p web camera struggles a bit with noise in low-light environments.

Verdict

The Lenovo IdeaPad Pro 5i Gen 9 (14-inch, 2024) is a stellar achievement in the mid-range premium segment, offering a near-perfect blend of performance, display quality, and battery endurance. It is an ideal companion for students, developers, and content creators who need a reliable workhorse without paying the premium tax of top-tier flagship lines.
