The gaming laptop market has shifted significantly towards thinner, lighter, and more versatile form factors over the past few years. Ditching the typical bulky chassis associated with portable gaming rigs, this laptop boasts a remarkably lightweight and clean aluminum design. Beneath its minimalist exterior lies a powerful combination of an Intel Core Ultra processor and dedicated NVIDIA RTX graphics. It immediately grabs attention with its gorgeous 2.8K OLED display, which offers vibrant colors and incredibly deep contrast ratios.
