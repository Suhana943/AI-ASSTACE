LG Gram Pro 16 (2024) Review: The Ultimate Featherweight Productivity Powerhouse

🛒 Buy Now on Amazon

The LG Gram Pro 16 (2024) stands as a testament to how far thin-and-light laptop engineering has evolved over the past few years. Weighing in at just under three pounds, this 16-inch machine challenges the convention that large-screen laptops must be heavy and cumbersome. Under the hood, it features the latest Intel Core Ultra 7 processor, delivering robust performance that effortlessly handles intensive office tasks and creative projects. The gorgeous 144Hz OLED display is an absolute joy to look at, offering deep blacks and vibrant colors that make both work and media consumption incredibly immersive. LG has also managed to pack a generous port selection into this ultra-slim chassis, including Thunderbolt 4, HDMI, and USB-A ports. While the chassis does exhibit a small amount of flex under heavy pressure, the overall build quality feels premium and highly durable. Battery life is another strong suit, easily pushing past the ten-hour mark under normal daily workflows without needing a top-up. The keyboard provides a comfortable, tactile typing experience, complete with a dedicated numeric keypad for data entry. For professionals and students who demand a spacious screen without sacrificing portability, this laptop represents an outstanding, top-tier choice.

⚖️ Verdict

The LG Gram Pro 16 (2024) sets a new benchmark for large-format ultraportables by masterfully balancing lightweight portability with high-end performance. It is the perfect companion for remote professionals who refuse to compromise on screen real estate.

💡 Pro-Tip: To maximize battery life even further when on the go, lower the display refresh rate from 144Hz to 60Hz in the Windows display settings during light productivity tasks.
