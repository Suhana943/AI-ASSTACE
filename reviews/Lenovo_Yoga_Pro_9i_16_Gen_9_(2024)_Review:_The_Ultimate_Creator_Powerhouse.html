✅ Pros

  • Breathtaking 165Hz Mini-LED display with exceptional color accuracy and HDR peak brightness.
  • Class-leading performance from the Core Ultra 9 and RTX 40-series GPU combination.
  • Stellar keyboard ergonomics, massive glass trackpad, and a robust 6-speaker audio system.

❌ Cons

  • Battery life is mediocre, especially when running the Mini-LED screen at high brightness.
  • Relatively heavy at 2.18 kg (4.81 lbs), making it less portable than standard ultrabooks.

⚖️ Verdict

The Lenovo Yoga Pro 9i (2024) is a spectacular triumph for content creators, video editors, and heavy multitaskers who refuse to compromise on screen quality and raw processing power. While its battery endurance won't break any records and the chassis carries some noticeable weight, the sheer performance overhead and unmatched thermal management make it one of the absolute best desktop replacements of the year.

💡 Pro-Tip: To maximize your battery life when working on the go, manually toggle the display refresh rate from 165Hz to 60Hz and disable HDR mode in the Windows display settings.
