🔍 Intel vs AMD: Kaun Sa Kam Budget Laptop Sahi Hai?

Jab aap ek naya laptop select karte hain, to processor hamesha sabse bada roll play karta hai. Chaliye dekhte hain dono me se aapko kaun sa chunna chahiye:

🟦 Intel Core i5 Kyun Chunein?

  • Single-core performance kaafi solid hota hai, jo MS Office aur regular apps ke liye fast hai.
  • Software optimization behtareen milti hai (Jaise Android Studio ya Adobe Suite).
  • Battery efficiency thodi behtar hoti hai daily tasking me.

🟧 AMD Ryzen 5 Kyun Chunein?

  • Ryzen processors me multi-threaded tasks (Jaise Video rendering aur Coding compilation) fast hote hain.
  • Iska Integrated Radeon Graphics Intel ke mukable thoda zyada gaming performance deta hai.
  • Heavy load ke daurani ye laptop thode kam garam hote hain.

🎯 Conclusion Advice:

Agar aapka main focus coding, documentation, aur official tasks par hai to laptop under 50000 in amazon par chal rahe HP Intel models ko chunein. Wahi agar aapko bade display par content consumption aur thodi editing karni hai, to ASUS Ryzen variant ek dumdar option hai.
