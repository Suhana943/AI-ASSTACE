Intel Core i5 vs AMD Ryzen 5: Asli Farak Kya Hai?

Under 50000 ke budget me sabse bada confusion Intel aur AMD processor ke beech hota hai. Dono hi behtareen hain, lekin dono ka kaam karne ka tarika thoda alag hai:

Feature Intel Core i5 (12th/13th Gen) AMD Ryzen 5 (5000/7000 Series)
Primary Use Single-core speed achhi hai (Office, Coding, Daily Browsing) Multi-core performance behtar hai (Video Editing, Multi-tasking)
Graphics (IGPU) Intel Iris Xe Graphics (Normal editing aur media ke liye sahi) AMD Radeon Graphics (Casual gaming aur rendering me zyada smooth)
Battery Life Moderate (Performance cores ki wajah se battery average milti hai) Excellent (Ryzen processors power-efficient hote hain)

Laptop Kharidte Waqt In 4 Baaton Ka Dhyan Rakhein (₹50k Budget)

Market me purane specs wale laptops ko saste me becha jata hai. Agli baar jab aap deal select karein, to ye parameters check karein:

  • RAM minimum 16GB honi chahiye: Aaj kal ke applications aur Windows 11 bina 16GB RAM ke lag karne lagte hain. 8GB RAM wale variants ab future-proof nahi hain.
  • SSD Type: Hamesha NVMe M.2 SSD wala laptop hi lein. Purani HDD (Hard Disk) wale laptops ko bilkul ignore karein.
  • Processor Generation: Agar Intel le rahe hain to 12th Gen ya usse upar hi lein (Example: i5-1235U ya 1240P). Purana 11th gen na kharidein.
  • Display Quality: Kam se kam Full HD (1920x1080) resolution screen honi chahiye. IPS ya Anti-glare panel ko priority dein.

Aapke Liye Kaunsa Sahi Hai? (Quick Conclusion)

Aapko Intel i5 lena chahiye agar: Aapko ek reliable laptop chahiye jisme software compatibility issues na hon, office ka heavy document work ho, coding karni ho, aur regular daily tasks bina kisi jhanjhat ke chalane hon.

Aapko AMD Ryzen 5 lena chahiye agar: Aap thodi bahut casual gaming (jaise GTA V, Valorant) karna chahte hain, ya fir aap YouTube ke liye 1080p video editing seekh rahe hain aur aapko lambi battery life ki zaroorat hai.
