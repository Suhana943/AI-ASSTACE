Intel Core i5 vs AMD Ryzen 5: Asli Farak Kya Hai?

Under ₹50,000 ke price bracket me sabse bada comparison Intel aur AMD chips ke beech hota hai. Dono power efficient hain par dono ki strength kaafi alag hai:

Feature Intel Core i5 (12th/13th Gen) AMD Ryzen 5 (5000/7000 Series)
Primary Work Behtar Single-core architecture (Coding, Single Tasks) Efficient Multi-core performance (Video Editing, Multi-tasking)
Graphics Integrated Intel Iris Xe (Normal graphics processing ke liye perfect) AMD Radeon Graphics (Casual gaming me continuous high FPS output)
Battery Optimization Moderate capability (P-cores heavy computation me load lete hain) Excellent performance (Ryzen chips inherently battery-saver hoti hain)

Laptop Kharidte Waqt In 4 Baaton Ka Dhyan Rakhein

Market standard configurations ke mutabik hamesha niche diye gaye parameters ko priority par check karein:

  • RAM minimum 16GB honi chahiye: Windows 11 ke high hardware demand ke chalte ab 8GB configurations future-proof nahi hain.
  • SSD Storage Architecture: Hamesha Fast NVMe M.2 SSD selection hi karein. Mechanical Hard Disk Drive (HDD) ko skip karein.
  • Processor Generation Cycle: Agar Intel variant le rahe hain to 12th Gen architecture se upar ka hardware hi prefer karein.
  • Display Layout Framework: Anti-glare or IPS panel layout screens ko choice banayein jiska minimum resolution Full HD (1920x1080) hona chahiye.
