1. Performance: Processor aur RAM ka Mel

Laptop ki raftaar uske "Brain" (Processor) aur "Memory" (RAM) par nirbhar karti hai.

  • Processor: Basic kaam ke liye Intel i5/Ryzen 5 best hain. Heavy creative tasks ke liye A18 Pro ya i7/Ryzen 7 zaroori hain.
  • RAM: Aaj ke multitasking zamane mein 8GB minimum hai, par smooth performance ke liye 16GB kaafi behtar hai.

2. Display aur Battery: Din bhar ka saath

Screen ki quality aur battery backup aapke user experience ko define karte hain.

  • Display: Hamesha IPS panel wala laptop chunein, isse colors acche dikhte hain aur aankhon par zor kam padta hai.
  • Battery: Sirf company ke claims par na jayein. Kam se kam 50Wh ki battery aapko 6-8 ghante ka real-world backup de sakti hai.

3. Storage: SSD ki Ahmiyat

HDD (Hard Drive) ka zamana gaya. SSD (Solid State Drive) aapke laptop ko 5-10 seconds mein on hone ki taqat deti hai.

Pro Tip: 256GB storage jaldi full ho sakti hai, isliye Cloud storage ya external drive ka backup zaroor rakhein.
