Laptop Kyun Important Hai?

Aaj ke digital daur mein, laptop ek zaroori tool ban gaya hai:

  • Portability: Aap kahin bhi baith kar apna kaam, padhai, ya gaming kar sakte hain.
  • Productivity: Office work, content creation, aur coding ke liye laptop ek powerful machine hai jo desktop jaisa performance deti hai.
  • Versatility: Yeh sirf gaming ke liye nahi, balki creative tasks aur rozana ke office kaam ke liye bhi upyogi hai.
